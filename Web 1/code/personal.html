About Me

My journey in technology began at SMKN 1 Cirebon, majoring in Software Engineering. This was the starting point where I learned programming logic, system development, and how to build applications from the ground up. The experience shaped my fundamental understanding of software development.

During my final year, I completed an internship at PT. Grage Media Technology as a Programmer and Content Creator. I contributed to the development of an HRIS system featuring face recognition attendance using Next.js as the frontend and Node.js as the backend. In addition, I was involved in producing several corporate YouTube contents, which strengthened my creativity and communication skills.

For my final school project, I collaborated with a team to develop a school news website using Laravel 11. This project demonstrated my ability to work in a team environment and apply modern web frameworks effectively.

Before entering university, I interned at Metland Hotel Cirebon as part of the IT Department. There, I expanded my knowledge beyond programming, including system maintenance, troubleshooting, and IT operations in a professional environment. As a final contribution, I developed a web-based work order system to replace manual handwritten reports, improving efficiency and documentation processes.

I also served as the Chief Executive of my school's graduation photo documentation project, where I coordinated teams and managed execution. This experience strengthened my leadership, responsibility, and organizational skills.

Currently, I am pursuing a degree in Informatics Engineering and have entered my second semester. I am committed to continuous learning and professional growth in the field of technology.
